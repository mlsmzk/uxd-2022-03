Phase 1: Introduction and Project Brief

Project Brief

We wish to pursue three avenues: adoption, pet sitting, and community building. Our end goal is to create an app that connects pet owners and animal enthusiasts that are looking to adopt, work, and/or play to build a sense of community.  

Probl​em

How can we make the process from adopting to owning a pet a more exciting, pleasant, and familiar/friendly experience?

Status Quo

Our project will:

  • Promote pet shelter adoption amongst communities
  • Facilitate and streamline the adoption process for potential owner/s and current (shelter, individual, etc.) owner/s
  • Provide support and a platform for connection between pet owners
  • Familiarize adoption seekers with potential pets
  • Centralize job postings
  • Add a dimension to the human-pet relationship
  • Lower the barrier of entry to the pet-care ecosystem (adoption, pet sitting, pet socialization, etc.)
Submission
    Currently, our naïve design idea consists of a mobile app. We want to explore the possibility of creating a seamless onboarding experience as well as a post-pet-adoption flow on a mobile app. We believe that there is a lack of incentive to adopt pets because of the long, tedious process, and we are aiming to enhance this experience. With a mobile app, we can deliver a quick onboarding experience that gets the user browsing and and connected to adoption institutions or pet-sitters (depending on use case). 

Information to b​e Collected

We will need to interview current pet owners (adoptee or not) to see if there is a desire for the 3 pathways of functionality that we want to deliver (adopt, sit, and party) or if they want a more focused app. We will also need to gauge how well current solutions are serving the intended target user.

Interview Targets: current pet owners, pet sellers, pet shelters, dog walkers, pet sitters

Team Work

Our team will split research, ideation, prototyping, and development of our mobile app evenly. We will collaborate with a focus to deliver one key experience, alongside other potential concepts.
