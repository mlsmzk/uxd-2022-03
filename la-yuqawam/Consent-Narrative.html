Consent Narrative

We are a team of User Experience Design Students at the Olin College of Engineering studying how to design software applications to be more user-friendly and human-centered. By the end of the course, we hope to create Adögi and make the process of rehoming, adopting and spending time with pets to be more convenient while maintaining similar, if not more, safety and health levels as shelters.

Who We Are

Purpose

We hope to promote the adoption of animals rather than buying from breeders through an app that streamlines the adoption process, while maintaining the proper safety and regulatory conventions.

Confidentiality

We will only share your name and contact information with the teaching staff. To protect your identity, we'll adopt pseudonyms and leave out any potentially sensitive material from our reports and presentations. We will remove all of your information from our user data if you ever decide not to continue with our project. Due to the fact that this user research study is being conducted by students we will not be able to provide you with any financial incentive and thus your involvement would be on a voluntary basis. We are very grateful for your assistance, and also hope for your enjoyment with this experience.
